# Privacy Policy for Word Chains

**Last Updated:** October 29, 2025

## Introduction

Word Chains ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application Word Chains (the "App") handles information.

## Information Collection and Use

### Data We Collect

Word Chains is designed with privacy in mind. The App:

- **Does NOT collect any personal information**
- **Does NOT track your activity**
- **Does NOT share any data with third parties**
- **Does NOT require account creation**
- **Does NOT access your contacts, location, or camera**

### Local Data Storage

The App stores the following information locally on your device only:

- **Game Progress**: Your current puzzle state and chain progress
- **Onboarding Status**: Whether you've completed the tutorial
- **Game Preferences**: Your selected word length and game mode preferences
- **Puzzle of the Day**: Locally stored daily puzzles

This data is stored using UserDefaults and never leaves your device.

## Third-Party Services

Word Chains does NOT use any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

## Children's Privacy

Word Chains does not knowingly collect any information from children. The App is suitable for all ages and does not contain any age-restricted content.

## Data Security

All game data is stored locally on your device using iOS's secure storage mechanisms. We do not have access to any of your data.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

## Your Rights

Since we do not collect any personal information, there is no data for us to access, modify, or delete. All game data is stored locally on your device and can be removed by deleting the App.
